About Me

I am currently a junior at Indiana University Bloomington, pursuing a degree in Marketing through the Kelley School of Business. I consider myself a determined and compassionate person with exceptional creative problem-solving skills. I excel at staying organized, adapting to various workplace environments, and building successful teams. My fascination with visual design has led me to take responsibility for imaginatively designing apparel for the Alpha Psi chapter of Pi Kappa Phi and coordinating with wholesalers to collect orders. I am passionate about the work that I do, and I look forward to translating my strengths into a future career.

I was born and raised in Chicago and through growing up in the city I have become independent and closely familiar with metropolitan culture from an early age. I attended the prestigious and incredibly diverse Whitney M. Young High School. Throughout my entire life, I have enjoyed the privilege of connecting with individuals of every ethnicity, sexual orientation, and economic status. Beyond the extensive experience and knowledge that I have to offer, I bring extraordinary interpersonal skills to the table. I seek to motivate and inspire all of those who cross my path by using my unique background and exceptionally refined communication skills to my advantage.

As I continue my journey to success, I would sincerely appreciate any suggestions, advice, or any stories you think would help me. Please reach out to me through any means of communication at any time and I will do my best to respond as soon as possible. Thank you.
